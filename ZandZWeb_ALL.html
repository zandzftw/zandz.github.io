Who are these guys?

Z&Z (Zack Grey & Zomgshiny) are a traveling duo of musicians, youtubers, and all around goofballs. They perform at conventions, breweries, and other venues with an urban folk style of music coupled with impromptu comedy improvs between songs. If you can't catch them at a show, check out their recorded music on their SoundCloud.

The boys also post videos of their exploits into gaming, music, and other adventures! You can find those on their Twitch and YouTube channels to enjoy the giggles and tunes as they post them there!

Just who are Z & Z?

ZomgShiny and Zack Grey are both guys from Florida. They have been on the scene for about 5 years now. They both come from different backgrounds but have become fast friends who share a love of music, adventure, and generally bringing entertainment to people they meet. They found a love of playing urban folk music while actively interacting with their audience and making a show that's half music/ half comedic banter. In the last few years they have found a niche playing shows primarily at conventions & breweries.

When they aren't traveling, they like to unwind with friends via online gaming, which often turns into crazy back-and-forths between themselves as well as their friends. So, they've decided to record those moments through Twitch and YouTube. They also post show footage, updates, and other things they think up as they come.





What is "Urban Folk?"

The quickest answer is:

"Listen to This!"

However, to get more specific, Urban Folk is pretty much folk storyteller music that takes place in more urban and contemporary settings instead of being the often sung and redone stories from old times. The actual sound of their music relies heavily on the boys natural harmonies along with guitar, percussion, and occassional harmonica in their live shows. The recordings are usually accompanied by more instruments, as the boys have the luxury of recording multiple tracks over themselves. However, the best way to know what their music sounds like is to just listen to it. And you can do that on their SoundCloud!

Who Does the Art?


"Neither of us!"

We can't stress that enough. We love the many talented and amazing artists who we have commissioned to bring our characters to life for the various songs, videos, and general lunacy we put them in. And as the list of artists have grown, so has difficulty in keeping track with who did what art for us when.

Still, we love them to bits and want them to be successful in the craft they work so hard to hone, so if you see a piece on our website and realize that you want art commissioned from that person, let us know by e-mail and we will get you in contact with them ASAP!



*And to any artists seeing your art on here. If you need or want it taken down, let us know. We appreciate your work on all of our pieces that we commissioned and respect any policies you may have on their use.
